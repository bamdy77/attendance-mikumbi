What TAMS Offers

Built for Accuracy & Simplicity

TAMS eliminates paper-based attendance with a smart, location-aware digital system that works on any smartphone.

GPS Verification

Teachers must be within 45 metres of the school to mark attendance. Location is verified automatically — no proxies possible.

Time-Window Enforcement

Registration opens at 6:00 AM and closes at 8:00 AM. Arrivals after 7:30 AM are automatically flagged as LATE.

Real-Time Dashboard

The Head of School sees live attendance data — who is present, absent, or late — from any device, anywhere.

Export Reports

Download attendance reports as Excel or PDF — daily, weekly, monthly, or for any custom date range.

Secure Authentication

Every teacher signs in with their full name and a personal password. Data is protected with JWT tokens and bcrypt encryption.

Works on Any Phone

No app download required. TAMS runs in any mobile browser and can be installed as a PWA — just like a native app.

Staff Announcements

The Head of School can send announcements to all staff instantly. Messages appear on the landing page for up to 3 days, can be removed at any time, and multiple announcements can be active simultaneously.

Simple Process

How Attendance Works

Marking attendance takes less than 30 seconds — from any smartphone, no paper needed.

1

Arrive at School

Come to school within the registration window — 6:00 AM to 8:00 AM every school day.

2

Open TAMS

Open the TAMS link on your phone browser or tap the installed app icon on your home screen.

3

Enter Your Details

Type your full name (3 parts) and your personal password. Allow GPS access when prompted.

4

GPS Verifies Location

The system confirms you are within 45 metres of the school automatically.

5

Attendance Confirmed

You receive an instant confirmation — ON TIME or LATE — and the record is saved to the database.
